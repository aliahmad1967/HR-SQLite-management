HR Management System - Installation Information
================================================

Thank you for installing HR Management System!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
----------------------

1. Default Login Credentials:
   Username: admin
   Password: admin123
   
   * Please change the password after first login!

2. System Requirements:
   - Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
   - 4GB RAM minimum
   - 500MB free disk space
   - Screen resolution: 1280x768 or higher

3. Data Location:
   - Database: [Install Dir]\Data\hr_database.db
   - Logs: [Install Dir]\logs\
   - Backups: [Install Dir]\Backups\

4. Features:
   - Employee Management (Add, Edit, Delete employees)
   - Attendance Tracking (Check-in/Check-out)
   - Leave Management (Request and approve leaves)
   - Payroll Processing (Salary calculations)
   - Department Management
   - Reports and Dashboard

5. Support:
   For support, please contact your IT administrator.
